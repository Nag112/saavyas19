Swachh Bharat

On the occasion of 70th Republic Day, Saavyas'19 NIT Goa had organised a cleanliness drive under Swachh Bharat initiative. This initiative was part of the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan whose aim is to clean up the streets, roads and infrastructure of India's cities, towns, and rural areas before 2nd October 2019 which marks the 150th anniversary of the birth of Mahatma Gandhi. The students of NIT Goa actively participated in the programme. They cleaned the surroundings as well as the interior of the campus. The garbage was then collected by local garbage trucks which eventually dumped it at dump site. Keeping the environment clean is not the duty of only the garbage collectors but the basic fundamental responsibility of every global citizen. As a student and as the young generation of India we should pledge to keep the environment clean and tidy. Such initiatives should be more prevalent so that more people can participate
