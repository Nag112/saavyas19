Ethnic Day

About Event


The Saavyas'19 organised its very first pre-event, Ethnic Day under the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat on 13th November 2018 at 5:30 pm near the Library Lawn of NIT Goa. The event was diligently organised. There was a soul to it. A soul that was vibrant, contagious and exuberant. Presided over by the Director of the college, Prof. (Dr.) Gopal Mugeraya, the function was attended by Ms Felly, a renowned cosmetologist and a member of the Art of Living Foundation based in Goa as the Chief Guest of the evening. Distinguished guests, faculty, staff and the students attended the function.
